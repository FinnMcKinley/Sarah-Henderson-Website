I love making jewellery that has the "wow" factor. Not being "every day" pieces, my jewellery deserves to be taken out somewhere swanky! I have been a jeweller for twenty years and learnt my trade as a bench hand for three talented jewellers over the course of ten years.
I love to push the boundaries with my design ideas. I work mostly with silver and gold and pride myself on its handmade value. A lot of my work can be worn in multiple ways and has an element of fun.
My aim is to make something that will always be in style using sleek lines and a quality finish.
